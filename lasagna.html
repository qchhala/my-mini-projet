How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 1 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Cover with foil and bake.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
